What this tour is about:

This tour will take you all across popular tourist locations throughout the city of Seattle. All locations are within a close enough location you could see all of them in real life in a day. I am from Bremerton which is a ferry ride away from Seattle so I have chosen some of my favorite spots in the city.

Places you are going to visit!

  • Space Needle
  • Pikes Place
  • Gum Wall
  • Mopop
  • Troll Bridge
