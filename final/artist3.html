A Brief History

Adrianne Lenker began working on her craft from a young age. She was born into a musical family and wrote her first song at age 8. During her teens she showed an interest in guitar and more specifically, finger picking. She went on to recieve a full ride scholarship to Berklee College of Music. During her time here, she focused her education on song writing while she continued to develop her finger picking style. in 2015 she formed indie folk band Big Thief. The band consisted of Buck Meek (guitar), James Krivchenia (drums), Max Oleartchik (bass), and Adrianne as the front women. Big Thief has had huge success both in the mainstream music world as they have recieved grammy nominations, but also critically. Adrianne also has had success in her solo carreer as she has released 5 studio albums, all met with praise from critics for their raw and emotional story telling and insight to her emotions.

Her Sound and Rec's

Adrianne Lenker has a very unique sound in the indie music world. She uses lots of metaphors and imagary to create very poetic lyrics that are full of meaning and emotion. She often explores themes like nature, love, and relatinships. Most of her solo work pairs her powerful lyricism with minimalistic accoustic guitar and her well known fingerpicking techniques. Adrianne's sound is intimate and creates a big atmosphere. From her solo work I would reccomend the album "Songs" and the song, "Ingydar".

Noticable Accomplishments

Paste magazine rated Adrianne Lenkers most recent solo album a 9.7 and called it a "near perfect album" (Link to the Article here). She has had huge success touring with an impressive cataloge featuring shows at Coachella and Pitchfork Music Festival. She is widely recognized as one of the strongest singer-songwriters today, with certain tracks like "anything" often used as examples of masterful song writing. Adrianne has become a figure head of the indie folk scene and has inspired a new generation of artists after her. Big Thiefs album "U.F.O.F" was grammy nominated for best alternative music album of 2019. This marked the mainstream success of Big Thief, with Adrianne Lenker at the figurehead.

Summary

Adrianne Lenker has become an icon of indie music for good reason. She has ammased a loyal and impressive fan base while still making high quality and honest art. Creating a minimalistic sound while still experimenting has led to her receiving massive respect in the music industry. Her early beginnings and longevity show her to be able of producing time less art. Her ability to create a full and embracing sound with Big Thief and then make a solo album of nothing but vocals and accoustic guitar shows her versatility as a song writer. Adrianne Lenkers authenticity to music and skill as a song writer will no doubt have her going down as one of the greatest artist of a generation.
