Questions

  • The dfference between inline and block tags is the normal flow. Block tags demand a whole line for their block while inline tags have the content right next to other inline tags.
  • Normal flow is how the tags in the html will produce space on the actual page.
  • A float can change how all information below it is positioned. Clearing it will end the floats control.
  • Relative is the space the original box was supposed to be. Absolute can pinpoint a location you desire. Fixed places a picture in a fixed location where scrolling won't chang the image.
  • You can make nice button areas by making them block tags instead of inline. You can also add padding and borders to make the area more defined.
