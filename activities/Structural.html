Welcome to my Web Development page

What goes in the header?

The header is used for creating a map for navigating the page. It is a tool for organization that puts whatever content you decide at the top of the page.

What goes inside the NAV?

The navigation tag is used for navigating the website. This can be the links you see to takae you to different pages. The Nav can be included inside the header but is not limited to it.

What goes in the Main?

The main contains the main content of your page. This is what is found below the header.

What goes in the footer?

Most footers contain a copyright. They can also contain links to other pages that may be useful to the user. Can also contain links for required legal reasons.
