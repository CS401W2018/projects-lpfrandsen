Questions

1. Inline tags go in line with the text. The block tags are what contains boxes and the inline tags only refer to the content.

2. The padding is the distance between the content and the border. The border contains the padding and content. The margin is the space beyond the border that gives space between other boxes.

3. The most common unit of measurement we will use is pixels or px. Pixels directly refer to the pixels on our screen and can be used to control size.There are several other units of measurements including, mm, cm, in, and even percentages.

4. You could use a figure tag to adjust the properties of an image. You can add borders, captions, and more.
